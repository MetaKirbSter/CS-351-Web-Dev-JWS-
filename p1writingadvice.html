What will you need to start your author's journey?

A table A laptop And your brain!

In all seriousness though, becoming a writer isn't easy! I would suggest incoming authors start with a small story to get their feet wet with publishers.

My first release was only about thirty pages. You don't need to create the next War and Peace as your starting point!

Don't forget to do practice drills to get yourself in the habit of spitballing quick ideas.

It's okay to take breaks! For every two hours you spend writing, give yourself at least a fifteen-minute break to get your bearings.
